Problem 12:
$$
r'(t)=(\sin(t),\cos(t),f'(t))
$$
$$
r''(t)=(\cos(t),-\sin(t),f''(t))
$$
$$
r'(t)\times r''(t)
\Rightarrow
\left\langle
\begin{array}{c}
\sin(t)f''(t)-\cos(t)f'(t)=c\\
\cos(t)f''(t)+\sin(t)f'(t)=c'
\end{array}\right.
$$
$$
(f''(t))^2+(f'(t))^2=c^2+c'^2=C^2
$$
and the solution of this differential equation is :
$$
f(t)=c_2-\frac{C}{\sqrt{\sec^2(c_1+t)}}
$$
and 
$$
f(t)=c_2+\frac{C}{\sqrt{\sec^2(c_1+t)}}
$$
